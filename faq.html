Frequently Asked Questions !

Whether you're new to the portal or need a quick refresher, we’re here to help you learn more
about our platform and its features.

  • Visit the official website.
  • Click on the "Register"button.
  • Enter your personal details, such as your full name, date of birth, ID proof, and contact information.
  • Create a secure password.
  • Verify your account using the link or OTP sent to your email or mobile.
  • Log in to your account.
  • Choose the vaccine you need.
  • Pick a convenient date and time.
  • Enter your health conditions.
  • Confirm the appointment.
  • LLog in to the portal.
  • Go to the “My Vaccination History” or “Certificates” section.
  • Click on “Download” next to your vaccination certificate.

The portal itself is free to use. However, vaccination fees may vary depending on the type of vaccine and your eligibility. You will be informed of any costs during the appointment booking process.

  • Visit the “Contaact Us” section.
  • Use the email support at immunizehub@gmail.com.
  • You can also call the customer service number listed on the website.
